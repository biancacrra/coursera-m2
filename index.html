Childhood favorite recipes

Recipe 1 - Bolinho de chuva:


Ingredients:
2 eggs;
1 cup of milk;
1 cup of sugar;
2 and 1/2 cups of all purpose flour;
1 tbs baking powder;
cinnamon and sugar to sprinkle on it;
frying oil.
_________________________

Preparation:
1 - Mix the ingredients until you obtain a creamy dough;
2 - Let the oil heat up in a tall pan enough that your dough can float;
3 - When the oil is heat to 180ºC (356ºF) start frying small amounts of dough, fry them until golden brown. 4 - Let the little "bolinhos" cool down on paper towel and finally sprinkle cinnamon and sugar on top of them.

Recipe 2 - Brigadeiro:


Ingredients:
1 can of condensed milk;
1 tbs butter;
3 tbs chocolate powder.
_________________________

Preparation:
1 - Mix the ingredients in a pan in medium heat until the Brigadeiro starts detaching from the pan.
Obs: you can shape them into little balls with chocolate sprinkles.

Recipe 3 - Passion Fruit Mousse:


Ingredients:
1 can of condensed milk;
1 can of table cream;
1/2 can of concentrated passion fruit juice;
_________________________

Preparation:
1 - In a blender, mix all the ingredients;
2 - Put the mousse in a platter in the fridge for at least 4 hours.
